National Central University (NCU)

A public research university in Zhongli, Taoyuan City, Taiwan.

  • Founded in 1915 in Nanking, it was reestablished in Taiwan in 1968 after the Chinese Civil War and gained its current name in 1979.
  • The university is organized into eight colleges, 26 departments, 19 graduate institutes, and six research centers.
  • It is one of the six national universities in research selected by the Ministry of Education.
